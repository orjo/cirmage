FM Screening Image Transformer

Upload an image and transform parts into exaggerated black and white FM screening

or drag and drop an image here

Instructions:

  • Upload an image using the "Choose Image" button or drag and drop
  • Adjust min/max dot size to control the size variation (flat areas = larger, detailed areas = smaller)
  • Adjust spacing to control gaps between dots (0% = tight, 50% = very airy)
  • Adjust contrast and threshold for the desired effect
  • Click and drag on the image to select a region
  • Click "Apply to Selection" to apply the FM screening effect to the selected area
  • Or click "Apply to Whole Image" to transform the entire image
  • Download your result when finished
